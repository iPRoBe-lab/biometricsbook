This website serves as a resource for the benefit of the readers of "Introduction to Biometrics". It includes additional reading material, links to public domain biometric databases and software, some homework problems and errata; we will make every attempt to keep it up to date. Readers' feedback and suggestions are most welcome and will help us update this page.
